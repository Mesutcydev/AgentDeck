AGENT/DECK Privacy

Last updated: July 19, 2026.

AgentDeck connects your iPhone to computers you explicitly pair. Session metadata and redacted event history are stored locally on your devices. AgentDeck does not require an AgentDeck cloud account to execute agents.

Data processed

Pairing identifiers, public-key fingerprints, connection endpoints, authorized project names, agent status, session events, approval records, diagnostics, and StoreKit entitlement status may be processed to provide the app.

Terminal and project data

Terminal output and project contents travel through the encrypted peer connection you configure. Diagnostics are redacted and exported only when you request them. Provider CLIs may process data under their own terms and privacy policies.

Purchases

Apple processes App Store purchases. AgentDeck receives verified entitlement information, not your payment-card details.

Contact

Privacy questions may be submitted through the AgentDeck GitHub repository.
