# App Review Notes (draft — §28)

AgentDeck is a remote control surface for Mac-resident coding agents. The iPhone never connects to agent protocols directly; it pairs with a user-operated Mac companion over TLS with mutual confirmation.

Demo flow:
1. Launch macOS Companion and complete onboarding.
2. Launch iOS app, scan companion QR code, confirm verification phrase on both devices.
3. Open Sessions to view structured agent timeline or raw terminal fallback.
4. Approve a sample approval from the Approvals tab (hold-to-confirm for critical risk).

No VPN, no public tunnels by default. Push notifications are optional and redacted.
